Unicorn

Unicorns are mythical creatures often depicted as white horses with a single spiraled horn on their foreheads. They symbolize purity, magic, and grace, and appear in legends, fairy tales, and fantasy stories around the world.

Dragons

Dragons are legendary creatures found in myths across many cultures, often portrayed as powerful, fire-breathing beasts with wings and scales. They can represent wisdom, strength, or danger, depending on the story and tradition
