The difference between Call Of Duty and Fortnite

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online battle royale game where players fight to be the last one standing. It features building mechanics, colorful graphics, and frequent updates with new skins and events.

Call Of Duty

Call of Duty is a bacfast-paced first-person shooter gameknown for its intense combat and realistic graphics. It offers various modes like campaign, multiplayer, and battle royale across different war-themed settings.
